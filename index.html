Successor of Hubble Telescope

Why need of James Webb Telescope?

The James Webb Telescope is powerful

It is the biggest, most powerful telescope ever to be put in space.

It will help us map the universe

The Webb telescope won't be able to decisively say there's definitely life on a planet or not, but it begins to map out that space.

It's a time machine of sorts

Hubble, when pushed to its maximum, could see galaxies that were teenagers in terms of age. We want to see babies.
